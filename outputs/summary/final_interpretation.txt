Final Interpretation - Weight Perception and BMI Percentile

The cleaned analysis dataset contained 12,853 students.

The highest mean BMIPCT was in the Very overweight group (92.30), while the lowest mean BMIPCT was in the Slightly underweight group (36.53).

The one-way ANOVA result was F = 1556.6455 with p-value < 0.0001. At alpha = 0.05, the decision was Reject H0.

Students' perceived weight category is associated with actual BMI percentile. This result shows association, not causation, because the YRBS data are observational survey data.
